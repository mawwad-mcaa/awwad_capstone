Responsible Tech Usage

People must be responsible with how they use tech. With how everything relies on tech in someway. Especially with the AI Boom. People need to be careful nowadays to not give their data to anyone. Or to do anything stupid like sending a virus to someone. You may never know what happens in the other side of the screen as the internet could be someone's life.

I used a lot of Mrs Lott's code in this as it is difficult to figure out code on your own. You will need a guideline in someway to help you out. In coding, there is a thing called the "Cliff of Confusion". It's when you're done with tutorials, and you're on your own. Because tutorials will always hold your hand. But, you need to not stress about it. Start small, and go big.
